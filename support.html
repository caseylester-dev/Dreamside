Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my sleep data showing up?

Dreamside reads sleep data from Apple Health. Make sure you've granted Dreamside permission to access Health data in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Dreamside. Sleep data requires an Apple Watch or another Apple Health-compatible device.

How do I generate a dream image?

Open a dream entry from the Gallery tab and tap the paintbrush button. Each account includes 5 free AI images. Subscribe to Dreamer or Lucid Dreamer for more.

Why did I get an error when generating an image?

Dreamside automatically simplifies dream descriptions that contain content that can't be illustrated directly. In rare cases, the imagery may be too intense even after simplification. You can add your own photo to any dream using the photo button on the dream detail screen.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. If you're still having trouble, contact us at the email above.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → Clear All Data. This permanently deletes all dreams, sleep data, and routines stored in the app.
